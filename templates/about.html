About this website

  • This website can recommend students material related to the digital system course and specifically the subjects of Numbering systems, numbering systems’ conversions and operations, 1’s and 2’s complement, Signed binary numbers, BCD numbers, BCD addition and logic gates.

  • The material our system recommends is based on the student's learning preference and the student's current knowledge about the subjects mentioned. It evaluates their preference based on the VARK model. It does this by using the VARK questionnaire after registration. As for the current knowledge, it uses a short quiz to determine the student's current level of knowledge in these subjects and then adapts to changes in this knowledge after the student has finished learning a new concept.

  • this website was created as a graduation Project submitted to the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in Birzeit University in partial fulfillment of the requirements for the degree of B.Sc. in Computer Engineering.
